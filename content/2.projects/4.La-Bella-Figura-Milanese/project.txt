<p>This research project was designed to capture contem-porary Milanese community by observing elderly fasion.</p> <p>
The topic of elderly fashion reflects various aspects of contemporary Milanese society such as ageing population, fashion and its relationship with deep rooted Italian philosophy of <i>La Bella Figura</i> [The Beautiful Figure - <br />the importance of one's presentation]. </p><p>
The project consisted of street fashion style photography, short interview in Italian and a final report on analysis of data gathered. These elements were compiled together in a small booklet. </p>
